About Lithora Exports

Leading Maharashtra's Natural Stone & Global Trading Excellence Since 1968

Our Legacy

A Journey Spanning Over 57 Years

1968

Business founded by Iranna Laxman Harlekar, initially dealing in timber and wood trading.

1972

Entry into natural stone segment with rough Shahabad stone (yellow limestone).

1980

Expansion into Tandur Grey stone and Kadappa Black limestone, strengthening presence in natural stone trading.

1997

Mr. Santosh Harlekar joined the family business and began aggressive diversification and modernization.

2003 Onwards

Direct exporting transition, supplying natural stones, Italian marbles, granites, and wallpapers across India and internationally.

Today

Among the few dedicated natural stone exporters from Maharashtra with a strong global footprint.

Who We Are

Lithora Exports is among the few established exporters from Maharashtra, with a legacy that began in 1968 as a family-run business.

Our Founder

Under the vision of Mr. Santosh Harlekar, the company transformed from a regional trader into an international exporter, acting as a trusted sourcing and trade partner for natural stones and allied materials across India and overseas markets.

Vision

To become Maharashtra's leading exporter of natural stones and global trade solutions through integrity, quality, and innovation.
